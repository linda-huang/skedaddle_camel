To install: 

`opam install Graphics`
`make build` 

To see the Camel and Enemy respond to keyboard movement, run: 
    `make demo` 
In this, you control movements with `w` `a` `s` `d` `q` `e` and quit with `0`.
After each movement, the window prints the new game round_state, with the updated
location and orientations of Camel and Enemies.   

To see a graphical representation of a randomly generated maze, run:
    `make maze`  
The Graphics window stays open until you press any key.
To change the size of the maze generated, change the arguments to the 
call to Draw_maze.main in the last line of Draw_Maze.ml (line 46).
Note precondition to Draw_maze.main.
